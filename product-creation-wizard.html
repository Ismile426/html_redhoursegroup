Catalog Explorer Product Listing Product Wizard

Built from the RedHorse Product Characteristics sheet. Only the characteristics that hold one value across the whole range are fixed on the Template. Anything the sheet lists with more than one value in a single range cannot be fixed — it becomes a configurable pool on the template that each Variant picks from. Some are derived (calculated once Ø & length are chosen). Each Variant gets its own item number (40400 → 404001), plus an optional add-on suffix (404001-1-12-2-3).
